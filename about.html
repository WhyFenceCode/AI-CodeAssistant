About Prompt Writing


THE ASTRONAUT


Astronaut in space, detailed matte painting, deep color, fantastical, intricate detail, splash screen, complementary colors, fantasy concept art, 8k resolution trending on Artstation Unreal Engine 5



When making a text prompt, the text is devided into two parts: The subject and The modifiers.



The Subject:

In the case of out prompt to the right, Astronaut in space is our subject. The subject tells the system what the art is that you want it to make. After the subject, I usualy put a comma.



The Modifiers:

In the case of the prompt, detailed matte painting, deep color, fantastical, intricate detail, splash screen, complementary colors, fantasy concept art, 8k resolution trending on Artstation Unreal Engine 5 is our modifier. The modifier tells the system what type of style you want. In some editors, such as nightcafe, adding the modifiers is a selection menu, so less typing!
